The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national-level competitive exam that primarily tests the comprehensive understanding of various undergraduate subjects in Engineering, Technology, Architecture, and post-graduate level subjects in Arts, Commerce, and Science.

âœ… Eligibility Criteria :

  • Currently in the 3rd year or higher of any undergraduate degree program.
  • Completed any government-approved degree program in Engineering / Technology / Architecture / Science / Commerce / Arts.
  • There is no age limit to appear for the GATE exam.

ðŸ§¾ Exam Details

  • Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Sections: General Aptitude (GA) + Candidate's Selected Subject.
  • Question Types: Multiple Choice (MCQ), Multiple Select (MSQ), and Numerical Answer Type (NAT).

ðŸ§­ Opportunities After GATE

  • Higher Education: Admission to M.Tech, M.E., M.S., or direct Ph.D. programs in premier institutes like IITs, NITs, and IISc.
  • PSU Recruitment: Direct recruitment into Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) like IOCL, ONGC, NTPC, etc.
  • Study Abroad: Admission to some foreign universities in Germany, Singapore, and other countries.
